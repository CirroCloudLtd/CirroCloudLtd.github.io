It includes familiar Microsoft Office applications such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook, as well as other online services like OneDrive, SharePoint, Teams, and more.Microsoft 365 brings together the best productivity solutions for you to stay connected, produce your best work and optimize performance, wherever you are. Whether you’re using a laptop or your mobile device, you can easily create impressive content, access important files, stay up-to-date, organized and collaborate with anyone from anywhere, anytime.

Microsoft Azure

Microsoft Azure is a cloud computing platform and set of services offered by Microsoft. It provides a range of cloud-based services, including computing, storage, analytics, and networking. Azure enables businesses to build, deploy, and manage applications and services in a highly scalable and available environment.Azure also offers a wide range of tools and services for developers, including Visual Studio integration, support for multiple programming languages, and a variety of APIs and SDKs. Additionally, Azure provides a robust ecosystem of partners and integrations, making it easy to integrate with other Microsoft and third-party services

Power Plaftform

The Power Platform is a collection of low-code/no-code tools and services from Microsoft that enables users to create custom business applications, automate workflows, and analyze data. It includes Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI, and Power Virtual Agents. Power Apps allows users to build custom business applications for web and mobile devices, without requiring extensive coding skills. It provides a range of pre-built templates and connectors to common data sources, as well as the ability to connect to custom data sources.
